About Me

Sometimes, life can feel overwhelming — you might find yourself anxious, stuck, low, or unsure of your direction or sense of who you are. These feelings can become stronger during periods of change, such as moving somewhere new, becoming a parent, changing jobs, leaving university, or navigating relationships.

As an integrative therapist, I offer a calm, compassionate, and confidential space where you can express yourself freely and be heard. Together, we explore your experiences and gently process challenges to support a deeper understanding of yourself, your needs, and what truly matters to you.

Through a collaborative and reflective approach, we work towards clarity, resilience, and self-connection — creating space to quiet the noise and support your growth.

How I Work

I offer sessions online and in person, drawing on person-centred, psychodynamic, and CBT approaches, tailored to your individual needs and what arises in our work together.

Online Sessions

Flexible online counselling sessions via secure video platform, offering convenience and accessibility from wherever you feel comfortable.

In-Person Sessions

Face-to-face sessions in a calm, private setting where you can explore your thoughts and feelings in a safe, confidential space.

Languages

Sessions available in both English and French, allowing you to express yourself in the language that feels most natural to you.
